# Zero-Shot Example

## Model used
GPT-5

## Prompt
Classify the following sentences as Positive, Negative, or Neutral:

1. I love going shopping, but I hate crowded places.
2. I hate junk food.
3. Playing soccer is my passion.
4. I like horror movies.
5. My gym is always crowded; however, I love working out.

## GPT-5 Response
1. Neutral (mixed emotions)
2. Negative
3. Positive
4. Positive
5. Positive

## Observations
In zero-shot mode, GPT-5 applied its own internal logic. Sentences with mixed cues (e.g., #1, #5) leaned positive or neutral depending on the modelâ€™s interpretation, showing limited control over criteria.
